What is e-Aadhaar?

e-Aadhaar is a password protected electronic copy of Aadhaar, which is digitally signed by the competent Authority of UIDAI.

What is Masked Aadhaar?

Mask Aadhaar option allows you to mask your Aadhaar number in your downloaded Aadhaar.

Aadhar Uses

Aadhaar serves as a primary identity proof for individuals, widely accepted across various sector
Aadhaar is used in the healthcare sector for identity verification and to avail health-related services.
