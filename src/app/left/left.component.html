Frequently Asked Questions

What is e-Aadhar?
e-Aadhar is a digitally signed Aadhar card that you can download from the official UIDAI website. It serves as a valid proof of identity.
How to update Aadhar details online?
To update Aadhar details online, visit the official UIDAI website and use the Aadhar Self Service Update Portal (SSUP). Follow the instructions to submit the required documents and complete the update process.
Can I download my Aadhar without a registered mobile number?
Yes, you can download your Aadhar without a registered mobile number. Visit the official UIDAI website, enter your Aadhar number, and follow the steps to receive an OTP on your registered email address for verification.
Is Aadhar mandatory for all services in India?
While Aadhar is widely used, it is not mandatory for all services in India. Some services may require Aadhar, but alternative identity documents are often accepted.
How can I check the status of my Aadhar update?
To check the status of your Aadhar update, visit the official UIDAI website and use the Aadhar Self Service Update Portal (SSUP). Enter the Update Request Number (URN) to track the status.
